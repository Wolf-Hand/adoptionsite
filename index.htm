Wright Adoption

Here's Our Story

Way back in December of 2021, we decided to grow our family through adoption! It was a long road with the happiest ending: we met our sweet son, Judah, in March 2023 when he was just a week old. We finalized our adoption in October 2023 and settled into life as a family of three!

There have been a few times over the last two years when we have considered growing our family again. Each time we ultimately decided we weren't ready to add a second child for a myriad of reasons.

Then, on January 24, 2025, we got a phone call from the agency who oversaw Judah's adoption.

Long story short: we are being blessed with the opportunity to adopt Judah's full biological sibling!!!!

Baby will be here in approximately 8 weeks! (late March or early April 2025)

We did not see this coming. We are woefully unprepared but SO, SO EXCITED!

We have the absolute best support system in the world, and we couldn't have gotten through Judah's adoption without all of you!

The scariest part of adopting has always been how to fund the adoption, and this time is no different. We have 8 weeks to raise $50,000.

We truly, deeply, appreciate any and all support, prayers, encouragement, or help you can send our way to bring home Judah's baby brother or sister!
