Wright Adoption

February 13, 2025

We have officially met our fundraising goal!!!

It's been less than 3 weeks since we shared that we were going to be adding baby #2 to our family!

Never in our wildest dreams did we think we would raise the money to fund this adoption so completely or so quickly!

There will never be adequate words to express how grateful we are to each person who has donated, purchased a shirt, gotten items on our registry, given us baby items, offered to watch Judah so we could get stuff ready, prayed (for us, baby, and birth mom), and everything in between!

We thank God for your generosity and love!

We are in complete shock of everyone's willingness to come along side us in this effort to love this sweet baby and bring them into our family!

All our love,

Ben, Lydia, and Judah Wright
