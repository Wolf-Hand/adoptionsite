Wright Adoption

February 19, 2025

We have made it to the great state of Washington! Baby is going to be born on the eastern side of the state this time so we are getting settled at a rental in Spokane!

Sweet bio mom is holding on but will deliver any day now! We want to be close by when that happens, thus our sudden departure. They have determined the original estimated due date of March 27 was way off. Doctor said yesterday that he believes she is at the 40 week mark now!

If baby girl decides to stay put over the next week, bio mom is on the schedule to be induced on 02/26! If you've been following along with us for a while, that date will probably sound familiar because that is also Judah's birthday!!!

We are thankful for all the people in our lives who have reached out and stepped up so that we could get out here as quickly as we did! Bosses, coworkers, friends, and neighbors have rallied around us in so many ways!

It really does take a village, and ours is the best!

We are *especially* thankful for Grandma Mary! She dropped everything to fly out here with us to help wrangle Judah so we can tie up loose ends for work and then focus on legal/medical considerations for baby girl! Truly a lifesaver!

All our love,

Ben, Lydia, and Judah Wright
