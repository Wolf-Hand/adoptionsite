Wright Adoption

January 27, 2025

It's hard to wrap my head around how blessed we have been in the few short days since we learned that our family will be growing!

We have officially raised over 20% of our overall goal!

AND we have a crib, a bassinet, a car seat, a double stroller (on the way), and all the 0-3 month clothes, bibs, and blankets we will need!

We are less than $2,000 away from overcoming our first hurdle which is wiring the retainer fee of $12,000 to the adoption agency. This will officially allow us to sign the contract and enter into the adoption agreement with the agency!

We literally could not do this without you!

Thank you, thank you, thank you!
